About Us

At AJR Organics we are dedicated to providing you with safe and nutritious bananas and all our banana products. As an Agro-Firm located in Warangal, Telangana, India, AJR Organics is dedicated to the supply of fresh and top-tier quality bananas to our clients. About Us AJR Organics offers a broad variety of bananas, such as fresh yellow Cavendish bananas and fresh green Cavendish bananas. Our bananas are grown through the method of organic farming, therefore they are pesticide and chemical-free. Besides fresh bananas, we also sell banana powder. Our banana powder is produced from premium quality bananas which are carefully dried and crushed to preserve their natural taste and nutrients.
