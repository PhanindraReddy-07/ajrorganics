About Us

At AJR Organics we are dedicated to providing you with safe and nutritious bananas and all our banana products. As an Agro-Firm located in Warangal, Telangana, India, AJR Organics is dedicated to the supply of fresh and top-tier quality bananas to our clients.

About Us AJR Organics offers a broad variety of bananas, such as fresh yellow Cavendish bananas and fresh green Cavendish bananas. Our bananas are grown through the method of organic farming, therefore they are pesticide and chemical-free.

Besides fresh bananas, we also sell banana powder. Our banana powder is produced from premium quality bananas which are carefully dried and crushed to preserve their natural taste and nutrients. Banana powder is a useful ingredient, which can be used in different kitchen jobs like baking, smoothies and baby food.

The top priority of AJR Organics is quality and freshness. We select and handle our bananas with care, to guarantee that they reach our customers in the best shape they could be in. If you enjoy fresh bananas as a snack or are looking for banana powder to be used in our recipes, AJR Organics is your go-to place.

Through the dedication to ecofriendliness and customer gratification, AJR Organics is determined to supply our customers with the best bananas and banana products. Contact us today to place your order and taste the natural health benefits of AJR Organics bananas.
